Hello!! I'm a Ph.D. student in linguistics at the University of California, Santa Barbara. I'm especially interested in the ways in which queer and trans folks use language in unique ways to construct identity and community, and in using computational models and methods to approach sociolinguitic problems. I recently finished my B.A. at the University of California, Berkeley in linguistics and computer science. While at Cal, I wrote a thesis on how to use computational models as an alternative method for identifying acoustic signals of queer speech, and worked as an editor on the Gender in Language Project.
